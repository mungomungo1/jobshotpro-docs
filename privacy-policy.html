Privacy Policy for JobShotPro

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

1. Introduction

JobShotPro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Business name (optional)
  • Phone number (optional)

2.2 Photos and Images

The app accesses your device's camera and photo library to:

  • Take photos of your work
  • Select existing photos from your device
  • Create marketing images with overlays
  • Save edited images to your device

Note: Photos are stored locally on your device and on our secure servers. We do not share your photos with third parties.

2.3 Project Information

Information you provide about your projects:

  • Project names
  • Client names
  • Location information
  • Project notes and descriptions

2.4 Usage Data

We may collect information about how you use the app:

  • Features you access
  • Templates you use
  • Platform sharing preferences
  • Error logs and crash reports

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the JobShotPro service
  • Create and manage your account
  • Store and process your photos and project data
  • Generate marketing images with your branding
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Your data is stored on secure servers
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With your consent: When you choose to share images to social media platforms (Instagram, Facebook, Google Business Profile)
  • Service providers: We may use trusted third-party services for hosting and analytics
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Modify: Update your profile information at any time
  • Export: Download your photos and project data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from promotional emails

7. Third-Party Services

JobShotPro integrates with third-party platforms for sharing:

  • Instagram
  • Facebook
  • Google Business Profile
  • Google Maps

When you share content to these platforms, their respective privacy policies apply. We recommend reviewing their policies.

8. Children's Privacy

JobShotPro is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Camera and Photo Library Permissions

The app requires access to:

  • Camera: To take photos of your work
  • Photo Library: To select and save images

You can revoke these permissions in your device settings at any time. However, this will limit app functionality.

10. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • As long as your account is active
  • For 30 days after account deletion (for recovery purposes)
  • Longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • In-app notification
  • Email to your registered address
  • Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

12. International Users

JobShotPro is operated in the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data:

Email: jobshotpro@gmail.com

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.
