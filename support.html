Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first post?

Tap the camera icon on the home screen to take a photo, or select one from your gallery. Then choose a template and let our AI generate captions for you.

How do I add my business logo?

Go to Settings > Business Profile > Upload Logo. Your logo will automatically appear on all your marketing posts.

How many posts can I create?

Free users get 3 posts per month. Pro subscribers enjoy unlimited posts plus all premium features.

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Tap on any premium feature or go to Settings > Subscription to view Pro plans and upgrade.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings > Subscription and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore any previous Pro subscriptions.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings and scroll to the bottom to find the "Delete Account" option. This will permanently remove all your data.
