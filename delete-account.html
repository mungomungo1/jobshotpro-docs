Delete Your JobShotPro Account

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

You can request deletion of your JobShotPro account and all associated data at any time. Once your account is deleted, this action cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the JobShotPro app on your device.
  2. Go to the Settings tab (gear icon in the bottom navigation).
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us at brianmungo@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and the email address associated with your account.

What Data Is Deleted

Data Type Action Timeframe
Account information (email, name) Permanently deleted Within 30 days
Photos and generated images Permanently deleted Within 30 days
Captions and post history Permanently deleted Within 30 days
Business profile information Permanently deleted Within 30 days
Subscription records Retained for legal/financial compliance Up to 180 days

Please note: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel any active subscriptions. Please cancel your subscription through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store before deleting your account.
